Follow along with the steps below to make your very own French Onion Soup!

    Ingredients:
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)
    How to actually make Chicken Parm:
  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, shaking off any excess.
  4. Dip the chicken into the beaten eggs, ensuring it's well-coated.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Coat each chicken breast with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere the crumbs.
  7. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a few tablespoons of olive oil.
  8. Cook the chicken breasts for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cooked through.
  9. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce.
  10. Place the cooked chicken breasts on top of the sauce.
  11. Spoon more marinara sauce over each chicken breast.
  12. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the chicken.
  13. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  14. Optionally, garnish with fresh basil or parsley before serving.
